ScoreStems provides tailored audio services for small to midsize productions and independent filmmakers. He is a composer and sound engineer with years of experience and an extensive background in audio. You can find more at oweneddy.com.

About the Founder

Owen Eddy

Owen Eddy is a composer, arranger, and audio engineer specializing in film and new music. His music has been described as having a “refreshing aura of optimism and openness” by Michael Parloff, retired principal flutist of the Metropolitan Opera Orchestra, as well as “attention-grabbing” and “clever” by Wolfram Koessel, cellist of the American String Quartet. You can find out more at oweneddy.com.
